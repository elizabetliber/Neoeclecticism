What is Notahash?
Notahash is a music project where the foundation of our creativity lies in musical encoding based on hash values and narratives in blockchain history. We are building a community by collaborating with musicians and artists to bring bold ideas to life and fostering close interaction with our early fans, where each individual will become a part of the project in the future.
The main advantage
of the Neoeclecticism token?
The main benefit of owning Neoeclecticism is the opportunity to collect a digital collectible item called 'Source' at a pre-sale price.

The first 20 owners of the ‘Source’ collection will receive a track converted based on the hash value of their wallet.
